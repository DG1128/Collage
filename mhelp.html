Frequently Asked Questions

How does Your House verify listings?

add remove

We have a multi-step verification process that includes confirming property ownership and checking for fraudulent activity. We encourage all users to visit properties in person before making any payments.

What are my responsibilities as a tenant?

add remove

As a tenant, you are typically responsible for paying rent on time, keeping the property clean and undamaged, and adhering to the terms of your lease agreement. Specific responsibilities may vary by location and lease.

How do I handle a maintenance request?

add remove

You should submit all maintenance requests through your Your House dashboard. This creates a documented record for both you and your landlord. For emergencies, please contact your landlord directly via the provided emergency contact number.
